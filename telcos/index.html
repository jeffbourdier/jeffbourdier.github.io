Working for a telephone company (or "telco," if you like) has given me a new appreciation for the telecom industry. After all, it was a telco (Bell Labs, to be specfic) that gave us UNIX, C, C++, and even some of the earliest music software. It has also led to some interesting developments in GIS (not to mention, of course, providing the essential services of long-distance communication and data transfer).

Given this newfound interest in telcos, I thought it might be instructive also to have some knowledge of the industry's fascinating history. This page is the summary of what I have learned, shared here (like my software) for the benefit of anyone else who might appreciate it. Enjoy!
