Who am I?

I am a software developer, musician, GIS/geospatial enthusiast, and proud husband and father of four precious children.

I develop software both by trade and as a hobbyist. My primary interests are GIS and music (especially MIDI). Professionally, my experience encompasses the following industries:

  • Energy (especially oil/gas pipeline)
  • Computer hardware/software
  • Telecommunication
  • Government
  • Academia

I currently work in telecom, for Frontier Communications. I'm not currently looking for a job, but if you want to see my résumé, click here.

As a musician, I have a collective 14+ years of experience performing a wide variety of styles, including classical, jazz, rock, and a capella, as a vocalist, bassist, and keyboardist.

Why am I on GitHub?

I am on GitHub primarily because I love writing software, and GitHub provides a convenient means of tracking and sharing my personal projects. Most of my projects on GitHub are developed mainly for my own use; they are hosted here in case they may be of benefit to anyone else. I also like to connect with other developers and see what they're working on.
