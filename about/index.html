Who am I?

I am a geographic information and computer scientist, musician, husband, and father of four children.

I write software both for a living (because I have to) and as a hobby (because I want to). Professionally, my experience encompasses mostly the telecom and energy (especially oil/gas pipeline) industries. I'm not currently looking for a job, but if you want to see my résumé, click here.

I'm not actively gigging, but I have a collective 14+ years of experience performing a wide variety of musical styles, including classical, jazz, rock, and a capella, as a vocalist, bassist, and keyboardist.

Why am I on GitHub?

GitHub provides a convenient way to track and share my FOSS projects. Although developed mainly for my own use, they are hosted here on GitHub in case they may be of interest to anyone else. I also like to connect with other developers and see what they're working on.
